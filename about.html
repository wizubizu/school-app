Divine Children Place International School (DCP) is a co-educational organization whose sole vision is to build leaders that are academically sound and spiritually filled for the development of our country.

DCP International School provides quality care and education for children from three months and above. Taking into consideration the peculiar needs of children, we have worked on making the school a vibrant, interactive and an enabling learning environment where pupils can have fun, thrive academically, and acquire leadership skills and other skills that are essential to their overall development.

At DCP International, academic excellence is our watchword. However, while holding on to her unchanging core value, we recognize the essential role that non – academic activities play in providing a holistic education in today’s world. Our rich extra-curricular activities provide our pupils with the opportunities to build their confidence, explore their creativity and talents and ultimately, create in them the awareness that through the development of their God given potential, they can achieve greatness in whatever pathway they choose.

The school has been structured to cater for various aspects of early year education. The main school, which has the Primary, is situated at 63 Tafawa Balewa Crescent off Adeniran Ogunsanya, Surulere. The School’s Annex, which has the Creche, Playgroup, Reception and Nursery, is situated at 32 Mba Street off Adeniran Ogunsanya, Surulere.

The Director, Mrs A.O. Osisami is a seasoned Educationist who has been in the educational sector for over a decade and is passionate about grooming leaders. She aims to grow DCP into a reference school in Surulere by God’s grace. Our management and teaching staff are highly competent, motivated and committed professionals who have requisite training in education.

The School also provides a safe and well-structured Afterschool Programme for children while their parents are at work and a Reading Club which holds every Saturday at the annex.
